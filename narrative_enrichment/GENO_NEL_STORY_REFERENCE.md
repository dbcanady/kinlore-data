### S09 · Thomas Boon and the Three-Dollar Coffin (~1765–c.1840s)

Thomas Boon was born ~1765 in Virginia. We know almost nothing about his early life — which is itself information, since in colonial America, the people who left no records generally owned nothing worth recording.
